Welcome to ROI-master GitHub Pages.

Roim is an adwanced image tool to extarct information out of medical images in DICOM format. The main goal of the project is to provide nuclear medicine specialist (both technologists and doctors) with versatile enough instrument to prepare and process studiesa results. Especially for the cases when due to technological or physiological details study results are far from awerages Thus "standard" clinical applications are not able to process them
